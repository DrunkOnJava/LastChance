Chapter I: Welcome to Blue Mountain

About Our Community

Welcome to Blue Mountain Property Owners Association (BMPOA), a unique mountain community nestled in the beautiful Shenandoah Valley of Warren County, Virginia. Our community, established in the late 1950s, offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, privacy, and neighborly connection.

Blue Mountain began as a vacation retreat for Washington D.C. area residents seeking respite from city life. Today, we are home to both full-time residents and weekend visitors who share a common appreciation for our mountain's natural beauty and tight-knit community spirit.

Our Mission

The Blue Mountain Property Owners Association exists to:

  • Maintain and improve community infrastructure
  • Preserve the natural beauty of our mountain environment
  • Foster a sense of community among property owners
  • Ensure the safety and well-being of all residents
  • Protect property values through thoughtful governance

Community Values

Respect for Nature: We are stewards of this beautiful mountain environment and work to preserve it for future generations.

Good Neighbors: We value privacy while maintaining a friendly, helpful community atmosphere.

Responsible Development: We balance property rights with community standards to maintain our mountain's character.

Chapter III: Community Life

The Lodge

Location: 540 Cliff Road, Linden, VA 22642

The Lodge serves as our community center and is available for rental by property owners in good standing. This beautiful facility features:

  • Main hall with capacity for 80 people
  • Full kitchen facilities
  • Stone fireplace
  • Restrooms
  • Wi-Fi internet access
  • Tables and chairs
  • Beautiful mountain views

Lodge Rental Guidelines

  • Events must end by 11:00 PM
  • No smoking inside the facility
  • Security deposit required
  • Cleaning checklist must be completed
  • Contact secretary@bmpoa.org to reserve

Deer Lake Recreation Area

Location: 3367 Blue Mountain Road, Linden, VA 22630

Our private recreation area features:

  • Sandy beach area
  • Swimming dock
  • Picnic tables
  • Fishing areas
  • Nature trails

Deer Lake Rules

Important: No lifeguards on duty - swim at your own risk

  • Recreational Area Pass required
  • Children under 16 must be accompanied by an adult
  • No glass containers
  • No motorized boats
  • Dogs restricted during summer season (Memorial Day - Labor Day)
  • Operating hours: Dawn to dusk

Fire Safety

Blue Mountain is designated as a HIGH FIRE RISK area. All property owners must:

  • Maintain defensible space around structures
  • Follow the 4 PM Burning Law during fire season
  • Never leave fires unattended
  • Have fire extinguishers readily available

Evacuation Routes

Know your evacuation routes BEFORE an emergency:

  1. Primary Route: Blue Mountain Road to Howellsville Road
  2. Secondary Route: Freezeland Road south to Linden
