Chapter I: Introduction & Governance

Welcome to Blue Mountain

The Blue Mountain Property Owners Association (BMPOA) is a vibrant mountain community nestled in the beautiful Shenandoah Valley of Warren County, Virginia. Our community, established in the late 1950s, comprises approximately 400 properties across 1,200 acres of pristine mountain terrain.

Annual Meetings

The BMPOA holds its annual meeting on the first Saturday of August at The Lodge. All property owners are encouraged to attend to stay informed about community matters and participate in important decisions.

Board Meetings

Regular board meetings are held on the second Monday of each month at 6:00 PM at The Lodge. All property owners are welcome to attend.

Fire Safety

⚠️ IMPORTANT: Open burning is FORBIDDEN AT ALL TIMES

This restriction is more stringent than Warren County regulations. Violations are a Class 3 misdemeanor with fines up to $500.

Bear Safety

  • Never feed bears or leave food outside
  • Secure garbage in bear-proof containers
  • Remove bird feeders during active bear season (April-November)
  • If you encounter a bear, back away slowly and make noise
