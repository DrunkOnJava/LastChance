Chapter I: Welcome to Blue Mountain

About Our Community

Welcome to Blue Mountain Property Owners Association (BMPOA), a unique mountain community nestled in the beautiful Shenandoah Valley of Warren County, Virginia. Our community, established in the late 1950s, offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, privacy, and neighborly connection.

Blue Mountain began as a vacation retreat for Washington D.C. area residents seeking respite from city life. Today, we are home to both full-time residents and weekend visitors who share a common appreciation for our mountain's natural beauty and tight-knit community spirit.

Our Mission

The Blue Mountain Property Owners Association exists to:

  • Maintain and improve community infrastructure
  • Preserve the natural beauty of our mountain environment
  • Foster a sense of community among property owners
  • Ensure the safety and well-being of all residents
  • Protect property values through thoughtful governance

Community Values

Our community is built on shared values that guide our decisions and interactions:

Respect for Nature: We are stewards of this beautiful mountain environment and work to preserve it for future generations.
Good Neighbors: We value privacy while maintaining a friendly, helpful community atmosphere.
Responsible Development: We balance property rights with community standards to maintain our mountain's character.

Chapter II: Governance

Board of Directors

The Blue Mountain Property Owners Association is governed by a nine-member Board of Directors consisting of five officers and four directors-at-large. Board members are elected by property owners and serve two-year terms.

Board Responsibilities

The Board of Directors is responsible for:

  1. Managing association finances and budget
  2. Maintaining common areas and infrastructure
  3. Enforcing community covenants and regulations
  4. Organizing community events and communications
  5. Representing property owner interests

Board Meetings

Regular board meetings are held on the second Monday of each month at 7:00 PM at The Lodge. All property owners are welcome to attend. Meeting agendas are posted 48 hours in advance on the community bulletin board and website.
